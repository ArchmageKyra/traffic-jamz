Traffic Accidents in Colorado

Explore traffic accident data in an innovative way with this tool! The data is a subset of the US Nation-Wide Traffic Data Kaggle, reduced to only the state of Colorado in the year 2019. Each accident is marked with a point (blue), which can cluster when zoomed in and show a heatmap when zoomed out. Below is a parallel coordinate plot to explore the data; filter by severity, weather condition, visibility, windspeed, and precipitation.

Click on a cluster to zoom in, and click on a point to see accident details.

Click and drag on any axis to select values within a range. Double click on a selected axis to reset.
