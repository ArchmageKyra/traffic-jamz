Traffic Accidents in Colorado

Kyra Slovacek, Mykael Pineda,Taylor Cunningham, Tyler Kemp
Made for Morteza Karimzadeh’s GEOG4043 Class
The purpose of this website is to visualize traffic accident data throughout Colorado in different weather conditions. The data was sourced from Kaggle on Nation-wide traffic accidents and was shortened to only include Colorado based accidents. The accident point data is colored by accident severity, while the clusters are colored by accident occurrence.
